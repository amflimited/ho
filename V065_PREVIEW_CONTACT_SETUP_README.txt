v065 Preview & Contact Setup Gate

Adds the backend bridge from triaged/researched business to preview setup and contact readiness.

New behavior:
- Prospects page has a Preview & Contact Setup prompt for the next available businesses.
- Business View has a single-business Preview & Contact Setup prompt.
- Prospects paste/import accepts setup_results JSON.
- Sales Research fallback also accepts setup_results JSON.
- Setup results are stored using existing allowed fields only.

No schema changes.
No scraping.
No preview.php.
No payment.
No outreach automation.
